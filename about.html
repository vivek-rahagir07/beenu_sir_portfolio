Biographical Insight

Beenu Kumar Taneja is a highly regarded corporate trainer, entrepreneur, and leadership development expert known for his extensive contributions to professional training, coaching, and organisational transformation in India.

With a career spanning nearly two decades, Beenu has emerged as a respected thought leader in leadership development, behavioural skills training, and performance enhancement across diverse sectors. His approach is rooted in practicality, behavioural insight, and empathy—methodologies that move beyond traditional lecture-based formats.

Drawing on his deepening expertise, Beenu made a pivotal move in 2012 founding Getting Roots Coaching & Training Pvt. Ltd., which has since become a prominent provider of tailored training solutions under his vision.
